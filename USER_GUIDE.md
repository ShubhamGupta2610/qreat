# RQEAT User Guide

Welcome to RQEAT - Your Online Restaurant Ordering Platform!

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account
1. Click **Sign In** in the top right corner
2. Click **Sign up** to create a new account
3. Fill in your details:
   - Username (letters, numbers, and underscores only)
   - Full Name
   - Mobile Number
   - Table Number (optional)
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Click **Sign Up** to create your account

**Note**: The first user to register automatically becomes an administrator.

### 2. Browse the Menu
1. Navigate to the **Menu** page
2. Use the category filters to browse:
   - Starter
   - Main Course
   - Dessert
   - Fast Food
3. Use the search bar to find specific items
4. View item details including price, description, and images

### 3. Add Items to Cart
1. Select the quantity using the +/- buttons
2. Click **Add to Cart**
3. View your cart by clicking the cart icon in the header
4. The cart badge shows the total number of items

### 4. Checkout and Payment
1. Review your cart items
2. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
3. Verify your customer information
4. Review your order summary
5. See any applicable loyalty discounts
6. Click **Pay with Stripe** to complete your order
7. You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure payment page
8. Enter your payment details (use test card: 4242 4242 4242 4242)
9. Complete the payment

### 5. Track Your Orders
1. Navigate to **My Orders** from the user menu
2. View all your past and current orders
3. Check order status:
   - **Pending**: Payment processing
   - **Received**: Order confirmed
   - **Preparing**: Kitchen is preparing your order
   - **Delivered**: Order is ready/delivered
   - **Completed**: Order fulfilled

### 6. Manage Your Profile
1. Click your profile icon and select **Profile**
2. View your account information
3. See your total spending
4. Track your loyalty rewards progress
5. Edit your personal information

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?
- Verify your username and password
- Remember: usernames are case-sensitive
- Contact support if you've forgotten your password

### Payment Failed?
- Check your card details
- Ensure you have sufficient funds
- Try a different payment method
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Order Not Showing?
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Verify you're logged in
- Check the "My Orders" page

### Discount Not Applied?
- Verify your total spending in your profile
- Check if you meet the minimum spending requirement
- Discounts are applied automatically at checkout
